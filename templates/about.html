Abstract:

Cryptocurrencies have become one of the most volatile asset classes within modern financial markets, influenced both by technical and market sentiment. Price prediction accuracy and sentiment analysis are crucial in facilitating informed decisions by investors, traders, and crypto enthusiasts. This platform leverages advanced AI techniques, including machine learning models for time-series forecasting and natural language processing for sentiment analysis, in delivering actionable insights. The system integrates real-time market data, sentiment trends, and technical indicators in order to create a comprehensive and interactive cryptocurrency analytics solution.
